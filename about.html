ABOUT ME

Hi I'm Natneam Mesele. I was born and raised in small town in Ethiopia - Degollo. When I was a kid I was interested in so many things starting from maths, physics, art, Philosophy, religion, science, astronomy ... literally everything I saw. When I was in grade 8, I went to my aunts place in Addis Ababa, which enables me to know what a computer is by playing with it all day. At the time I didn't find it that much interesting because I was playing videos games all day.

In my high school we were having an ICT class. Even if it was about microsoft office suite it was some how interesting. When I'm in 11th grade I find my old friend, who was attending university, told me about programming and problem solving stuffs they are learning in his class. He thought me the basics of c++ as well. And that's when the journey to become a software developer started. Now I'm second year software Engineering student in AAiT. I want to have career on cyber security besides becoming professional software Engineer.
